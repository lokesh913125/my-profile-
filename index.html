about me

The story so far.

From a small town in Chhattisgarh, building global-quality products with nothing but vanilla JS, late nights, and a stubborn refusal to use frameworks.

I'm Lokesh Patel, a 22-year-old self-taught developer from Chandrapur, Chhattisgarh. Currently in the final year of my B.Com at Chandrapur College (SNPV University, Raigarh) — but my real classroom is the browser console.

I build complete, deployable products end-to-end: browser games, AI-powered tools, Hindi document apps, and fitness trackers. No frameworks. Just HTML, CSS, vanilla JS — single-file builds shipped to Vercel.

What makes my work different: I build for India first. Hindi UIs, Razorpay payments, Indian foods in the fitness DB, IPL career mode in the cricket game. Then I take those products global.

Right now I have 4 live monetized apps, 6 more on GitHub, and 10 in the pipeline. The goal? Go full-time as an independent developer — and ship the HOPE manga universe along the way.
